Your 12-week program

Three phases: Base, Load Progression, Sport Transfer. Each is four weeks. A phase unlocks once you've logged enough sessions in the one before it, so there's no rushing ahead on paper.

Exercises adapt as you go

Log how a session felt, and individual exercises will suggest an easier or harder version: not the whole week, just the ones that need it. You decide whether to take the suggestion.

Everything you log, tracked

Sessions, sets, reps, load, effort: all saved on this device only. Check Progress for charts and trends, and mark milestones (first full-pace crutch sprint, first club session) as you hit them.

What this app is, and isn't

The conditioning program is built on established strength & conditioning principles. Sport-specific skills (ball control, crutch agility) are left for you to fill in from a real coach or club session. This app won't invent technique instruction it isn't qualified to give.
